About Me

Yuan-Kang (Neil) Lee is currently an Image Quality Algorithm Engineer at Mediatek, specializing in development of camera image quality algorithms. His research focuses specifically on image quality assessment, low-level image processing, and computer vision. He received his Master’s degree from the Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering at National Taiwan University. Prior to joining Mediatek, he was a Camera Imaging Engineer Intern at Logitech and an Image Processing Engineer Intern at Robert BOSCH GmbH.
